What We Can Draft for You

Need documents generated for a client? Here's what your firm can hand off to DocForge. You focus on counsel — we'll handle the drafting, generation, and polish.

Important: DocForge provides professional document drafting and generation services as support staff. We are not attorneys and do not provide legal advice. All documents generated by DocForge must be reviewed, assessed, and approved by a licensed attorney before execution. Our work product is designed to save your firm time — not replace your professional judgment.

Whether it's a straightforward revocable trust or a complex irrevocable planning instrument, we've got the expertise to draft it. Just assign us the matter — we'll generate the full package, review it for accuracy, and hand it back ready for your attorney's final sign-off.
