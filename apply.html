Join Our Team

Interested in Becoming a Drafter?

We're building a team of skilled paralegals and legal assistants who love trusts, estates, and probate work. If that sounds like you, we want to talk.

What We're Looking For

We're building a distributed team of contract drafters. You'll work remotely, take on as much or as little volume as you want, and be part of a small team focused on quality and expertise. This isn't timesheets — it's per-package work. Take on the projects that interest you, turn them around right, and get paid.

Requirements

  • Paralegal certificate, legal assistant experience, or equivalent
  • Familiarity with estate planning documents
  • Strong attention to detail and formatting skills
  • Ability to work independently
  • Reliable internet and quiet workspace
  • Trustate Certified or willing to get certified
  • Experience with legal intake platforms

How It Works

When a client assigns us a matter, we distribute the work to our drafters. You pull the package, work through it, and send it back for review — all at your own pace. There's no time tracking, no minimum hours, no timesheets. You get paid per package completed. The work is flexible, the expectations are clear, and the team is small enough that you actually know who you're working with.

We care about quality and expertise, not volume. If you're the kind of paralegal who loves doing estate planning work right, we want to work with you.

We review every application personally. Expect to hear back within 3–5 business days.
