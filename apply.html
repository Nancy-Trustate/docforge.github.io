Join Our Team

Interested in Becoming a Drafter?

We're building a team of skilled paralegals and legal assistants who love trusts, estates, and probate work. If that sounds like you, we want to talk.

What We're Looking For

DocForge partners with law firms to handle their document drafting and generation through the Trustate platform. We need contract drafters who can work independently, communicate clearly, and deliver polished legal documents without hand-holding. This is remote, flexible, contract work — you set your own hours and take on as much or as little as fits your schedule.

Paralegal certificate, legal assistant experience, or equivalent background in trusts, estates, and/or probate
Familiarity with estate planning documents — revocable trusts, irrevocable trusts, wills, POAs, trust funding, trust admin, probate filings
Strong attention to detail and document formatting skills
Ability to work independently and meet deadlines without constant oversight
Reliable internet connection and a quiet workspace
Trustate Certified or willing to complete Trustate certification (we'll help you get there)
Experience with legal intake platforms (Decision Vault, Clio, Wealth Counsel, etc.)

How It Works

We operate on a contract basis. When a law firm sends us a matter, we assign it to a drafter based on availability, expertise, and turnaround needs. You draft the documents in Trustate (inside the firm's own account), run the quality review, and deliver. You get paid per completed package — no hourly tracking, no timesheets, no micromanagement. Just good work, done well.

We're a small team right now, and that's intentional. We'd rather have a handful of excellent drafters than a crowd of mediocre ones. If you're the kind of person who triple-checks formatting and catches the typo everyone else missed, you'll fit right in.
