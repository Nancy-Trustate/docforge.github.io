What We Forge

Services

Legal Document Drafting

Expert drafting of estate planning documents, operating agreements, trusts, probate filings, and more. We work directly within your workflows to deliver accurate, ready-to-use documents.

Trustate Document Generation

We know Trustate inside and out. From running the drafting question sets to generating complete document packages, we handle the entire generation process so your team doesn't have to.

Quality Review & Polish

Every document gets a final review before it reaches your desk. We check formatting, consistency, and presentation to make sure everything looks polished, professional, and client-ready.

Matter Sync & Data Transfer

No matter what intake platform your firm uses — Decision Vault, Clio, Wealth Counsel, or others — we can work with your data sheets and sync matters into Trustate seamlessly. No bottlenecks, nothing lost in translation.

Built on Trustate

Trustate Experts. Platform Fluent.
