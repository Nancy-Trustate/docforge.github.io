About DocForge

DocForge, LLC is a Washington State legal document drafting company founded on the principle that precision and efficiency should go hand in hand. With a background in paralegal work and legal technology, we bring both the legal knowledge and the technical savvy to deliver documents that are accurate, professional, and built to last.

We work with businesses, law firms, and individuals who need reliable document drafting without the overhead of a full legal department.
